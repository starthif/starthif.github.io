Are you planning a trip to New York? You are in for a treat! New York is a city full of life and adventure. From the iconic skyline to the bustling streets, there is something for everyone. To help you make the most of your trip, here are the top 10 tour spots you must visit when traveling to New York. 

## 1. Empire State Building 

Address: 350 5th Ave, New York, NY 10118

The Empire State Building is one of the most iconic landmarks in the world. It stands 1,454 feet tall and offers stunning views of the city from the 86th and 102nd floors. Take the elevator up to the observation deck and enjoy the breathtaking views of the city. 

## 2. Times Square 

Address: Broadway & 7th Ave, New York, NY 10036

Times Square is one of the most popular tourist attractions in New York. It is a bustling area full of bright lights, street performers, and shops. Take a stroll through the area and enjoy the vibrant atmosphere. 

## 3. Central Park 

Address: Central Park, New York, NY 10024

Central Park is a beautiful 843-acre park in the heart of Manhattan. It is a great place to escape the hustle and bustle of the city and enjoy some peace and quiet. There are plenty of activities to do in the park, such as biking, boating, and ice skating. 

## 4. Statue of Liberty 

Address: Liberty Island, New York, NY 10004

The Statue of Liberty is one of the most iconic symbols of freedom in the world. Take a ferry to Liberty Island and get up close and personal with the iconic statue. You can also take a tour of the island and learn about its history. 

## 5. Brooklyn Bridge 

Address: Brooklyn Bridge, New York, NY 10038

The Brooklyn Bridge is one of the oldest and most iconic bridges in the world. Take a walk across the bridge and enjoy the stunning views of the city skyline. You can also take a boat tour of the bridge and get a unique perspective of the city. 

## 6. High Line 

Address: Gansevoort St & Washington St, New York, NY 10014

The High Line is an elevated park in the heart of Manhattan. It is a great place to take a leisurely stroll and enjoy the views of the city. There are plenty of restaurants and shops along the way, so you can take a break and grab a bite to eat. 

## 7. 9/11 Memorial 

Address: 180 Greenwich St, New York, NY 10007

The 9/11 Memorial is a tribute to the victims of the September 11th attacks. It is a somber reminder of the tragedy that occurred on that day. Take a moment to reflect and pay your respects at this memorial. 

## 8. Rockefeller Center 

Address: 45 Rockefeller Plaza, New York, NY 10111

The Rockefeller Center is a complex of 19 buildings in the heart of Manhattan. It is a great place to take a stroll and admire the architecture. You can also visit the observation deck on the 70th floor and enjoy the stunning views of the city. 

## 9. Grand Central Terminal 

Address: 89 E 42nd St, New York, NY 10017

Grand Central Terminal is one of the most iconic train stations in the world. It is a great place to take a break and admire the stunning architecture. You can also take a tour of the station and learn about its history. 

## 10. Broadway 

Address: Broadway, New York, NY 10036

Broadway is the home of the world-famous theater district. Take a stroll along the street and admire the stunning architecture. You can also catch a show at one of the many theaters in the area.
