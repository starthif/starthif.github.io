Are you planning a trip to New York? If so, you're in for a treat! New York is one of the most exciting cities in the world, with a plethora of attractions and activities to explore. From iconic landmarks to hidden gems, here are the top 10 tour spots you must visit when traveling to New York. 

#1. Empire State Building 

The Empire State Building is one of the most iconic landmarks in New York City. Standing at 1,454 feet tall, it is the tallest building in the city and offers breathtaking views of the city skyline. The observation deck is open 365 days a year, and visitors can take in the sights from the 86th and 102nd floors. Address: 350 5th Ave, New York, NY 10118. 

#2. Central Park 

Central Park is an 843-acre public park in the heart of Manhattan. It is the most visited urban park in the United States and is home to numerous attractions, including the Central Park Zoo, the Belvedere Castle, and the Central Park Carousel. Visitors can also enjoy a range of activities, such as biking, boating, and ice skating. Address: Central Park, New York, NY 10022. 

#3. Statue of Liberty 

The Statue of Liberty is one of the most recognizable symbols of the United States. Located on Liberty Island, the statue stands 305 feet tall and is a symbol of freedom and democracy. Visitors can take a ferry to Liberty Island and explore the grounds, as well as take a tour of the pedestal and the museum. Address: Liberty Island, New York, NY 10004. 

#4. Times Square 

Times Square is a major commercial intersection and entertainment hub in Midtown Manhattan. It is home to numerous attractions, including the Broadway Theater District, Madame Tussauds wax museum, and the MTV studios. Visitors can also enjoy a range of street performers, restaurants, and shops. Address: Times Square, New York, NY 10036. 

#5. Brooklyn Bridge 

The Brooklyn Bridge is a suspension bridge that connects Manhattan and Brooklyn. It is one of the oldest and most iconic bridges in the United States and offers stunning views of the city skyline. Visitors can take a walk across the bridge and explore the nearby DUMBO neighborhood. Address: Brooklyn Bridge, Brooklyn, NY 11201. 

#6. High Line 

The High Line is an elevated park that runs through the Chelsea and West Village neighborhoods of Manhattan. It is a popular spot for locals and visitors alike, offering stunning views of the city and a range of activities, such as walking, biking, and bird watching. Address: High Line, New York, NY 10011. 

#7. 9/11 Memorial and Museum 

The 9/11 Memorial and Museum is a tribute to the victims of the September 11th attacks. It is located at the site of the former World Trade Center and features a memorial plaza with two reflecting pools, as well as a museum with artifacts and stories from the attacks. Address: 180 Greenwich St, New York, NY 10007. 

#8. Rockefeller Center 

Rockefeller Center is a complex of 19 commercial buildings in Midtown Manhattan. It is home to numerous attractions, including the Top of the Rock Observation Deck, Radio City Music Hall, and the NBC Studios. Visitors can also enjoy a range of shops, restaurants, and seasonal events. Address: 45 Rockefeller Plaza, New York, NY 10111. 

#9. American Museum of Natural History 

The American Museum of Natural History is one of the largest and most popular museums in the world. It is home to a range of exhibits, including the Hall of Human Origins, the Hall of North American Wildlife, and the Hall of African Mammals. Address: Central Park West & 79th St, New York, NY 10024. 

#10. Coney Island 

Coney Island is a beachfront neighborhood in Brooklyn. It is home to a range of attractions, including the iconic Cyclone roller coaster, the New York Aquarium, and the Coney Island Boardwalk. Visitors can also enjoy a range of restaurants, shops, and seasonal events. Address: Coney Island, Brooklyn, NY 11224. 

From iconic landmarks to hidden gems, there is something for everyone in New York. Whether you're looking for a fun day out or a relaxing getaway, these top 10 tour spots are sure to make your trip to New York unforgettable.
